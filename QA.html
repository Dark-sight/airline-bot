✈️ Flight Ticket FAQs

How to change my flight ticket? ▼

You can reschedule your flight through:
1. "My Bookings" section on our website
2. Customer service hotline +1-800-123-4567
3. Airport service counter (minimum 2hrs before departure)
* Change fees vary by fare conditions

What's the baggage allowance? ▼

Economy Class: 1 checked bag (≤23kg) + 1 carry-on (≤7kg)
Business Class: 2 checked bags (≤32kg) + 2 carry-ons (≤7kg)
Excess baggage: $15/kg for international flights

Child ticket requirements? ▼

• Infants (0-2yrs): 10% of adult fare (lap child)
• Children (2-12yrs): 75% of adult fare
• Valid ID required (birth certificate/passport)
• Maximum 2 infants per adult

How to request special meals? ▼

1. Select during booking process
2. Manage booking section (up to 24hrs pre-flight)
3. Call our customer service
Options: Vegetarian, Halal, Kosher, Diabetic, etc.

Pet travel regulations? ▼

• Requires 72hr advance notice
• Health certificate mandatory
• IATA-approved travel kennel
• Max combined weight: 50kg
• Breed restrictions apply
